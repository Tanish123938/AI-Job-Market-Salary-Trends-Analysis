# AI-Job-Market-Salary-Trends-Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes AI job market demand and salary trends using MySQL for data storage or to get insights and Power BI for interactive visualization. The goal is to understand how AI-related roles vary across job titles, experience levels, locations, and salary ranges, helping users gain clear insights into the current AI job landscape.

## Objective

- Analyze AI job roles and salary distribution
- Identify high-paying AI roles and experience-based trends
- Visualize job demand across locations and job types
- Provide data-driven insights for students and job seekers

## Key Features

- Salary analysis by job title and experience level
- Location-wise AI job demand
- Comparison of average, minimum, and maximum salaries
- Interactive Power BI dashboard with filters and slicers
- Clean and professional visual design for easy interpretation

## Insights

- Senior-level AI roles show significantly higher salary ranges
- Certain locations offer more AI opportunities with better pay
- Machine Learning and Data Scientist roles dominate the market

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates the effective use of MySQL and Power BI to analyze real-world job market data and convert it into meaningful insights. It is ideal for showcasing data analytics, visualization, and database skills.
